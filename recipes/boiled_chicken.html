Boiled Chicken

My dog Oliver's favorite food of all time.

My dog Oliver has digestive issues so we like to give him chicken as a treat and he loves it.

Items needed:

  • Chicken Breast
  • Pot or Saucepan
  • Strainer
  • Cutting Board
  • Knife

Steps in cooking

  1. Put chicken in a pot, cover it with water, and place it on the stove.

  2. Cover the pot, turn it up to high heat, and bring it to a boil.

  3. Let it cook according to the appropriate cooking time referenced above.

  4. Turn off the heat and drain the chicken in a colander.

  5. Place the chicken on a cutting board and check it with a kitchen thermometer to ensure it reaches 165Â°F.

  6. Allow the chicken to cool to room temperature.

  7. Chop the chicken into bite-size pieces or shred it with a fork.

  8. Set aside enough chicken to feed your dog and place the leftovers in an airtight, sealed container.

  9. Serve according to instructions from your veterinarian.

  10. Store the rest in the fridge.
