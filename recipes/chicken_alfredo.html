Chicken Alfredo

My favorite food of all time.

This recipe is a pretty standard reipe for my all time favorite food.
When I came home from Afghanistan this was the very first thing I wanted to eat.

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine
  • Chicken Breast
  • Herbs & Spices
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Heavy Cream
  • Parmesan Cream

Steps in cooking

  1. Cook the pasta just until al dente. Season chicken with olive oil, herbs, and spices.

  2. Cook chicken in a large saute pan for about 4-7 minutes per side or until a golden brown crust forms on the outside and the chicken is cooked through on the inside.
    Let chicken rest for 5 minutes, then slice.

  3. Cut butter into 8 slices, add to the large skillet, and reduce heat to low. Add garlic and cook for 30-60 seconds, stirring frequently. Do not allow garlic to brown!

  4. Whisk in heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer over medium heat.

  5. Reduce heat to low or medium-low and simmer gently (do not boil!), whisking frequently, for about 3-4 minutes or until it thickens slightly. It should easily coat the back of a spoon.

  6. Remove the pan from heat and stir in Parmesan cheese, nutmeg, salt, and pepper.

  7. Stir until the cheese melts.

  8. Add cooked pasta and toss to coat evenly. Season to taste with salt and pepper.

  9. Add chicken and return to medium-low heat for a couple minutes or until everything is warmed through.

  10. Garnish with chopped parsley (and a pinch of red pepper flakes if you like) and serve!
